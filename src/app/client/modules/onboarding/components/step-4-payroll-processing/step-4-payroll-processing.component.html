To ensure sufficient time for direct deposits and shipment of paychecks, payroll must be received by Oasis prior to 11 a.m. EST/PST, a minimum of 2 business days prior to check date. Clients paying via wire require a minimum of 3 business days prior to check date.

Tips

Charge tips are to be paid on Oasis issued check and cash tips are for reporting only. If your pay frequency is greater than weekly, hours and tips are required to be reported on a weekly basis for accuracy of half-time calculations.

Service Charges are mandatory amounts designated by a company to be included in the billing for the actual service. These are considered amount owed by the customer and not a voluntary gratuity (i.e. an 18% automatic service charge is included on a restaurant bill).
